The Almighty Sun Test

The Almighty Sun

Description

This is a game that I made for the PyWeek 35 Game Jam, the theme was "In the shadows", it is not a masterpiece, but I am happy with it, I might work on it in the future to make it better. The game is basically a "survivor" game, or at least that's what it's supposed to be.
You're in a desert and you need to find a radio to escape, your water levels drop over time due to the sun, so your only hope is to hide in the shadows to survive.
Keep in mind, this was a game for a 7 days game jam and I only could use 2 of those days, so there is a lot of improvement to do.

A short gameplay of the game:

How to Play

Move with the UP, DOWN, LEFT and RIGHT arrows.
Press ESC to quit the game.

Watch your water level, it will drop down with time.
The sun will make you thirsty, so try to avoid it and hide in the shadows.
To refill your water level you can search for water bottles.

Find the RADIO to escape the desert
